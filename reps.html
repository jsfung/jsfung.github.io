Reps

Runners have enjoyed the fluid fitness experience with applications where they just enter a few pieces of information and it passively tracks your workout, but that fluidity of tracking doesn't exist for weight lifting. Our goal with Reps was to make tracking your weight lifting exercises as seamless of a task as possible.

Existing weight lifting applications require you to manually insert all of your sets and reps, and don't provide a simple rest tracker. When you're exercising, you don't want to constantly be checking the clock, or having to enter every single rep and set. You want to focus on the exercise.

Reps automatically tracks your exercises as you're doing them. You tell it what exercise you're doing, and it can detect each rep you do and add it to your total. Then with a tap on the phone held in your armband or pocket, you automatically enter into your rest period so you know exactly how long you have before your next set. As soon as it's time for your next set, Reps lets you know.

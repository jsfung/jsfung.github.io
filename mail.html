CHALLENGE

The goal of a conceptual model is to communicate a concept within an object or graphic that a person can view or interact with to understand the process of idea the model represents. In this exercise, I began by exploring different concept maps that dove into deep detail on a single subject before generalizing, or conceptualizing, on a single point to provide a high-level view.

CONCEPTS

In ideating to decide on a final subject to form a model about, I explored three different ideas. I began with a detailed process map, and honed in on specific points of the map to provide a more high level picture. I explored the use of process maps to show instruction, venn diagrams to show relationships, and maps to use space to show the amount of impact items have.

Concept One: Ramen
Ramen is a staple of college students, but it can also become a gourmet dish. There are different ways to enjoy the meal, depending on your soup base and toppings, or you may choose to stick with your budget-friendly cup noodle.

Concept Two: Curing Hiccups
In all of our lives, we've heard different methods to cure hiccups, and I thought it might be fun to explore all the different ways a quick Google search would provide in a process map. I created a quick visual displaying the instructions on my own trusted cure.

Concept Three: Mail
Nearly everyone loves receiving handwritten letters and postcards, but it is funny how as we grow older and receive more mail, the amount of this type of correspondence from friends and family doesn't grow as well.

OUTCOME

In my final digitized conceptual model created in Adobe Illustrator, I chose to focus on the idea of mail as we age over time, as there is a soft spot in my heart for paper mail. By forming a system model that combines maps to use physical space to show amount of impact and a matrix to show distinction between categories, I represented how the volume of mail we receive increases as we become older, but the quality of the mail also drops significantly, as we see that the amount of spam mail and bills increase while more meaningful pieces like personal mail decreases.
